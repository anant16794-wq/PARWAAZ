Mrs. World

Sargam Koushal, from Jammu, became the Mrs World 2022, bringing the title back to India after 21 years, a significant success story for the region and the nation. A teacher-turned-model, her victory inspired many women to pursue their dreams and was celebrated by her local community in Jammu.
